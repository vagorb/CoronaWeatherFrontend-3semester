About CoronaWeather

Our goal at CoronaWeather is to provide people with easy to use way of getting the most relevant information about the weather and the corona virus situation in our world

Using our app provides you the following opportunities:

Find information about weather at the moment by searching the desired city.

Give some suggestion for user about clothes and things to take with you, when you are going outside based on weather conditions of the searched city.

Deliver information about situation with corona virus (total cases, deaths, new daily cases and etc.) in country, city of which you searched for.

Also You can get information about weather for 7 upcoming days at the place, what you searched in first point.

For users, who live in small or unknown cities, with what OpenWeatherMap(API) is not familiar, search weather and information about corona virus based on coordinates.
For that you need to log in and fill the simple form(City(or custom name for this area), country, latitude, longitude).
After that you can find up-to-date weather and corona virus information about that spot on daily basis by simply searching by city or spot name in the app.

Logged in users will be automatically provided with information about weather and corona virus situation in their hometown.

Find out about interesting places by looking through the most searched cities in the app.

Also app has admin feature, which will provide opportunity to delete existing info from database(weather forecast).

Our app has user friendly design with minimum buttons and only necessary information.
